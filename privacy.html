Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Poker Tracker mobile application.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Our application operates entirely completely offline. All data entered into the app—including player names, email addresses, buy-in amounts, and chip counts—is stored locally on your device's internal storage. The developer does not collect, transmit, intercept, or store any of this data on external servers.

2. Use of Email Addresses

The app allows users to input email addresses for the sole purpose of generating game summaries. The app does not send emails in the background. Instead, it generates a pre-formatted text template and securely passes it to your device's native email client (such as Gmail or Apple Mail). The developer has no access to these email addresses, the contents of the messages, or your contact list.

3. Third-Party Data Sharing

Because the app does not collect any user data off-device, absolutely no user data is shared with, sold to, or accessible by third parties.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
